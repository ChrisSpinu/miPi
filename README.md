# miPi
Minimal MP3 player, based on the RP2354A, with an e-ink display and that can double as a media controller.

<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-30 200306" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/51e524f9-ecea-40b0-8e6e-cae99d5f69a0" />
The MP3 player part will be handled directly by the RP2354A, outputing audio through two of its GPIO pins, while the radio receiver part of it will be handled by a RDA5807m module.
